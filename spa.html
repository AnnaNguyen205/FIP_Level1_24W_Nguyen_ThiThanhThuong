Summary of our Facilities

Welcome to our Spa Activities page, where you’ll find everything you need to know for a blissful experience at our spa.

We take pride in offering 6 Spa Treatment Rooms to cater to your relaxation and rejuvenation needs. Upon arrival, we recommend wearing loose and comfortable clothing for facials, while for Body Molding and Body Wraps, you’ll don a robe and an undergarment, with the option to change in the Treatment Room for added comfort.

Our appointments are available from 9:00 am to 9:00 pm, accommodating up to 2 people at a time based on accommodation size and Spa Practitioner availability. Please note that cancellations and no-shows made less than twelve (12) hours prior to the appointment time will incur a 100% charge of the full treatment price.

To ensure a successful Algonquin Spa experience, we encourage pre-booking your appointment. While we strive to accommodate every appointment, unforeseen circumstances like illness may arise. Rest assured, we’ll notify you promptly and work diligently to find alternative options for your booking. Additionally, please be aware of our NO EXTRA TIPPING policy, as your satisfaction with another appointment, activity, drink, or extended stay is our priority.

REGISTERED MASSAGE THERAPY

Touching is healing; from a mother's touch which comforts and soothes her baby to the triumphant athlete patted on the back by fellow athletes. Based on the same principle as human touch, massage is the oldest method of promoting good health, suppleness and strength. Today, the role of massage therapy is recognized by the general public and other health care professionals as being an effective health care alternative. Due to public perception and desire, massage therapy has grown into a more prominent position. It is a viable and respected profession promoting good health not only for your physical but emotional well being also. Massage Therapy is the kneading, manipulation or application of methodical pressure and friction of the body. Whether it is for treatment of injury, stress or for relaxation, the skilled use of massage through touch is used to maintain and enhance the health and well being of the entire body. Experience the benefits massage can offer you.
