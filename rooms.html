CHATEAUS

Chateaus at Couples Resort

Nestled amidst the enchanting landscapes of Old European Boutique Chateaus, each offering a Private Screened in Gazebo, Walkout Balcony, and Outdoor Hot Tub, these havens invite you to unwind and immerse yourself in the tranquility of nature. Overlooking the picturesque Galeairy Lake and the captivating forests of Algonquin Park, these retreats offer a serene escape to soothe life’s burdens away, surrounded by breathtaking views that enchant in every season.

As you journey across the globe, it becomes evident that few experiences rival the splendor found here. From your private Outdoor Hot Tub, set on a deck designed for stargazing, to the towering 16-foot vaulted ceilings and a magnificent two-sided stone fireplace adorning the spaces between the bedroom and living room, every detail speaks of luxury. Furnished by Canada’s esteemed furniture emporium, The Art Shoppe of Toronto, these accommodations blend opulence with nature’s beauty, creating a haven where every moment is a celebration of life’s finest offerings.

VILLAS

Villas at Couples Resort

Nestled lakeside, the Old European Boutique Spa Villas beckon with their private screened-in gazebos and individual outdoor hot tubs, offering a secluded retreat in nature’s embrace. These intimate sanctuaries are designed to spoil you with water indulgences, including in-room whirlpool bath tubs, saunas, steam rooms, and four shower heads, creating a haven of relaxation and rejuvenation.

Indulge in the luxury of a romantic private cottage for two, transformed into a Boutique Spa Villa that promises to pamper your senses. From the tranquil setting of your gazebo and sun deck, immerse yourself in the soothing sounds of water lapping at the shore, the gentle sway of trees in the breeze, and the rejuvenating experience of being enveloped in nature’s beauty. Each villa is uniquely designed in an Old European Boutique style, adorned with furnishings from Toronto’s renowned “The Art Shoppe,” Canada’s premier furniture store, ensuring a blend of elegance and comfort in every corner. Daily inspections ensure that every aspect of your stay, including the outdoor hot tub, is meticulously cared for, allowing you to unwind and savor every moment of your retreat.

Exotics at Couples Resort

Indulge your adventurous spirit in the HOT TUB EXOTIC Jr. SUITES, designed to awaken the tribal essence within you. These suites are tailored for those who dream of a romantic escape filled with wet indulgences and exotic experiences. These carefully curated details ensure that every moment in the HOT TUB EXOTIC Jr. SUITES is a celebration of adventure and relaxation, leaving you refreshed and rejuvenated.
